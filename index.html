Tania Nijhawan, BDS
Founder, Udana Wellness

Dr. Tania Nijhawan, an accomplished entrepreneur and Cosmetic Dentist, holds the esteemed position of Director at Nijhawan Group, a preeminent Indian travel conglomerate boasting a rich legacy spanning over four decades. The group, with a global presence, represents distinguished airlines such as British Airways for an impressive 34 years and collaborates with major hotel chains. In addition to its core travel business, Nijhawan Group is actively engaged in venture capitalism and angel investments.

 

Dr. Nijhawan’s academic prowess is exemplified by her BDS degree from Dr. D. Y. Patil Dental College & Hospital, specializing in Aesthetic Dentistry. Beyond her successful dental career, she co-founded ‘Fork ‘N’ Spoon,’ a renowned catering service operating under the umbrella of Nijhawan Group.

 

Diversifying further into the food industry, Dr. Nijhawan assumes the role of Director at ‘Nuts For Us,’ an enterprise specializing in premium dry fruits, seeds, and cold-pressed oils. With a remarkable 130-year legacy, ‘Nuts For Us’ caters to the discerning tastes of luxury retail consumers, providing health-conscious and visually appealing gifting options.

 

Alongside her entrepreneurial endeavors, Dr. Nijhawan maintains a strong commitment to dental aesthetics. She serves as a visiting Cosmetic Dentist in premium dental centers in Delhi, contributing to the enhancement of people’s confidence through the creation of beautiful smiles. Dr. Nijhawan’s multifaceted expertise seamlessly blends business acumen with a passion for healthcare, making her a distinguished figure in both the corporate and medical arenas.
